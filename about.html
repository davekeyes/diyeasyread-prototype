How do I check my Easy Read translation?

You should check your Easy Read document before you share it.

1. Check the meaning

  • Does the Easy Read say the same thing as the original?
  • Are any important details missing or changed?
  • Is the main message clear from start to finish?

Tip: Read both versions side by side.

2. Check the words

  • Are sentences short?
  • Are there any hard words that should be removed?
  • If a hard word needs to be included, does it have an explanation?

3. Check the layout

  • Does each sentence have only one idea?
  • Is there plenty of empty space on the page?
  • Are headings clear?

4. Check the pictures

  • Do the pictures help explain the words?
  • Are they respectful?

Tip: If the image is not quite right you can choose a different one or upload your own.

5. Check tone and respect

  • Does the document talk to the reader and not about them?
  • Is it respectful?
  • Does it support decision-making and independence?

6. Ask someone else to review it

  • Ask a person who uses Easy Read to check your translation
  • Ask an Easy Read expert to check your translation
  • They can tell you if it makes sense and feels right

When your document passes these checks you are ready to share it!
